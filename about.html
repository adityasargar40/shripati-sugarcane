About Us

Our History

SHRIPATI SUGAR & POWER LTD was established in 1985 in the fertile region of Daflapur, Maharashtra. What began as a modest sugar processing unit has grown into an integrated sugar and power generation facility that serves as a pillar of the local economy.

Over the decades, we have continuously invested in modernizing our facilities, improving our processes, and expanding our capabilities to meet the growing demands of our customers and the changing landscape of the industry.

Our Mission

To produce high-quality sugar and renewable energy while supporting sustainable agricultural practices and contributing to the economic development of our region.

Our Vision

To be recognized as a leader in the sugar industry, known for our innovation, sustainability, and commitment to excellence in all aspects of our operations.

Our Core Values

Integrity

We value our role in the community and are dedicated to supporting local development and prosperity.
