Frequently Asked Questions

How can I sell my sugarcane to SHRIPATI SUGAR?

Farmers interested in selling sugarcane to us can register with our Agricultural Department. We provide support throughout the cultivation process and ensure fair pricing based on quality parameters.

Do you offer factory tours?

Yes, we offer guided tours of our facilities for educational institutions, business partners, and other interested groups. Please contact our office to schedule a visit with at least one week's advance notice.

What are your minimum order quantities for sugar products?

Our minimum order quantities vary by product type and packaging. For retail packaging, the minimum order is typically 1 metric ton, while for industrial orders, we usually require a minimum of 5 metric tons.

Do you export your products?

Yes, we export our sugar products to several countries. Our export department can provide detailed information about our export capabilities, certifications, and shipping arrangements.
